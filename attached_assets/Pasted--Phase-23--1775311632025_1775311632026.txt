الحكم الرسمي المؤقت
اكتبه هكذا:
The galaxy-history block still does not deliver a clean explanatory variable in the standard sense. However, a semi-circular family of rotation-curve irregularity proxies survives MHI control with significant permutation support. This points to kinematic/structural irregularity rather than classical stellar-population history as the first genuinely independent signal.
